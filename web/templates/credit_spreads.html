Understanding the Metrics

ROC Return on Capital (Credit / Max Loss)
Delta Short leg delta (lower = further OTM, higher win rate)
Win% Estimated probability of profit at expiration
BE Break-even price (PCS: profit if price stays above BE, CCS: profit if price stays below BE)
AI Score AI-powered composite ranking based on ROC, liquidity & slippage
